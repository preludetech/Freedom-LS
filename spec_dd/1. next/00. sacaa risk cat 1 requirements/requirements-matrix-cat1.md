# SACAA 141.07.1 — Requirements Matrix (Risk Category 1 only)

Filtered view of [`requirements-matrix.md`](./requirements-matrix.md) showing only requirements that apply at the lowest risk category (Cat 1). Source: [`sacaa-requirements.md`](./sacaa-requirements.md).

These are the universal requirements — they apply to every e-learning system regardless of risk category.

## Section 1 — E-Learning systems (Cat 1 rows)

| ID | Requirement (plain-language gloss) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1(a) | Identification + password as a minimum security feature, with stringency scaled per the risk matrix. | Rule says "as a minimum" and "in alignment with the risk matrix" → applies universally; higher Cats require stronger auth. |
| 1(f) | Programme information must have a backup or be stored on different servers. | Universal — no Cat restriction in text. |
| 1(g) | Keep a register of course revisions/changes safely, preferably with an additional backup. | Universal. |
| 1(i) | Trainees must be given adequate time to complete training (except for timed modules). | Universal. |
| 1(l) | The programme must require learner interaction every 2 min 30 s, or auto-logout the student. | Universal. |
| 1(n) | An instructor or subject-matter expert must be available to assist the learner. | Universal. |
| 1(o) | Well-organised courseware with menus, modules, and instructions. | Universal. |
| 1(p) | Information flow must build knowledge, skills, and abilities in a logical order. | Universal. |
| 1(q) | Usability (software, human-computer interaction, hardware) must be a primary consideration. | Universal. |
| 1(r) | The programme should include audio and visual instructions. | Universal. |
| 1(x) | Identity management and authentication must be built into the system. | Universal — even Cat 1 (self-declaration) is a form of identity claim. |
| 1(y) | The system must be hosted within the Republic (of South Africa) and have information-protection mechanisms. | Universal. |
