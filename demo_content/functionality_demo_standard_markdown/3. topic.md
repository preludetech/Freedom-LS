# COMPOUND INTEREST

Why is compound interest the real wonder of the world?

## What is compound interest?

Compound interest is the interest calculated on the initial principal and also on the accumulated interest of previous periods of a deposit or loan.

<c-callout title="Callout">
Let us work with an example of R1000. 10% interest per annum. For 10 years.
</c-callout>
