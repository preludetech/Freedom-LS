Callouts are one of our custom Cotton components.

**A component for displaying informational alerts and notices.**

They support a `level` attribute. Which determines how it looks.

Supports levels: *info*, *warning*, *error*, *success*

They also support a title attribute. Which determines the top level heading.

Inside the callout tag we support normal mark down content.


# How to use:

<c-callout level="info" title="Info Callout">
    This box has a title.

    Callouts are a great way to break up long walls of text.
</c-callout>

<c-callout level="info">
    This box stands alone without a header.
</c-callout>

<c-callout level="success" title="Success Callout">
    Success! You can proceed to graduation.
</c-callout>

<c-callout level="warning" title="Warning Callout">
    Warning has been logged.
</c-callout>

<c-callout level="error" title="Error Callout">
    **THIS IS IMPORTANT INFORMATION**
</c-callout>


## Cheat Sheet:

`<c-callout level="info" title="Info"> ... </c-callout>`

`<c-callout level="success" title="Success"> ... </c-callout>`

`<c-callout level="warning" title="Warning"> ... </c-callout>`

`<c-callout level="error" title="Error"> ... </c-callout>`
