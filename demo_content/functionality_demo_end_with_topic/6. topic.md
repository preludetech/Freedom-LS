Pictures are one of our custom Cotton components.

**A component for embedding YouTube videos responsively.**

They support a `video_id` attribute. This is the unique string found at the end of a YouTube URL (not the whole URL).

They also support a `video_title` attribute. This is used for accessibility (screen readers) and does not display visually on the video itself.

## How to use:

<c-youtube video_id="8EMyP2pesB0" video_title="DjangoCon US 2024: Keynote - Power to the People who Teach the People with Sheena O'Connell"></c-youtube>

<c-youtube video_id="5qap5aO4i9A"></c-youtube>

### Where to find the Video ID?
If your URL is [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ), the ID is the part after v=: dQw4w9WgXcQ.

## Cheat Sheet:

`<c-youtube video_id="..." ></c-youtube>`

`<c-youtube video_id="..." video_title="..." /></c-youtube>`
